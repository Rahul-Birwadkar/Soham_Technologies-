Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, process, and pricing.

What types of industrial equipment do you repair?

+
We repair Servo Motor Drives (AC & DC), VFDs, Electronic PCBs, PLCs, HMI Touch Panels, CNC Controllers (Fanuc/Siemens/Mitsubishi), Elevator Drives, Excavator ECU/ECM, Fire Alarm Systems, Industrial Monitors, Power Supplies/SMPS, Encoders, Control Panels, Crane Joysticks, Shearing & Bending Machines, Robot Spares, and Hydraulic Equipment — 25+ service categories in all.

How long does a typical repair take?

+
Standard turnaround is 2–5 business days depending on the fault complexity and parts availability. Express service is available for critical production needs. We always provide a clear timeline estimate before starting work.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

+
Yes — all repairs carry a 90-day warranty covering the same fault. If the identical issue recurs within the warranty period, we repair it at no charge. We stand completely behind every repair we do.

Which brands do you service?

+
We service Siemens, ABB, Fanuc, Yaskawa, Mitsubishi, Allen Bradley, Omron, Delta, Bosch Rexroth, Schneider, Vacon, Danfoss, Lenze, Parker, Komatsu, Caterpillar, JCB, Hyundai, Fatek, Rockwell, Emerson, GE Fanuc, Control Techniques and 100+ more brands across all industrial categories.

How much does industrial repair cost?

+
SMPS repairs start from ₹2,500. Drive and controller repairs typically range ₹5,000–₹50,000 depending on the unit, saving you 60–80% vs OEM replacement cost. We provide a free diagnosis and transparent quote before any work begins — no hidden charges.

Do you offer on-site repair services?

+
Yes — we offer on-site machine maintenance and repair across Mumbai. For large installations or equipment that cannot be transported, our engineers can visit your facility. Contact us via WhatsApp or call to arrange an on-site assessment.

Can you repair PCBs that others call unrepairable?

+
Yes — we specialise in complex PCB repairs with a 98% success rate. Our engineers perform true component-level diagnosis using professional test equipment, which enables us to fix boards other workshops cannot handle.

How do I send my equipment for repair?

+
You can courier or hand-deliver to our workshop at Hanuman Industrial Estate, G D Ambekar Marg, Mumbai 400031. Alternatively, WhatsApp us to arrange a pickup. Always include a written fault description with the unit for the fastest processing.
